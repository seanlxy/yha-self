How to get here

Access to this hostel is easy and you can get here by via car bus or shuttle. Shuttle/Bus: Buses stop about 600m from the hostel. Most shuttles pick up at the hostel; please get in touch with hostel staff for advice or to book. The main Bus Interchange in Christchurch is approximately a 15 minute walk from the hostel

overview

Perfect for schools and large groups! Find out more

YHA Christchurch Rolleston House is a characterhome which has been converted into a comfrotable hostel located opposite the Canterbury Museum, the Botanical Gardens and Hagley Park. Stroll along the willow-lined Avon River or enjoy Hagley Park's acres of green Parkland.

Rollesston House is centrally located and is handy to bus routes. It's the perfect home base for exploring everything Christchurch has to offer: the city, the beaches, the Port hills and everywhere else you might like to wander.

YHA Christchurch is always busy with fun activities offered on site. Please book in or check at the reception to participate in the activities

Monday: We head on a walk to the gardens and the city centre.

Tuesday: Come along to the Canterbury Museum ($12 entry for YHA members) and take a free guided tour of the historical museum.

Wednesday: Join us for a movie night in the hostel, popcorn included. Free!.

Thursday: Meet & mingle with other guests on our City walking tour..
